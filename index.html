About Us

At KoppattiAmman Wood Works, woodworking isn’t just a trade — it’s a cherished tradition passed down through three generations. Rooted in the heritage of Tamil Nadu, we take pride in blending age-old techniques with today’s design sensibilities to craft furniture that speaks to both history and modern living.

From modular kitchens and wardrobes to wooden beds, sofas, chairs, dining tables, and pergolas, every piece we create reflects a harmony of timeless skill and contemporary style. Our craftsmanship extends to aluminium channel partitions, wooden windows and doors, staircase guard rails, TV showcases, and elegant dressing tables — all custom-built to match your needs and taste.

Every detail is a tribute to our roots, yet designed for the future. When you choose KoppattiAmman Wood Works, you're not just furnishing a space — you're bringing home a legacy of artistry, tradition, and lasting quality.
